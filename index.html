What I do

Researcher

I have previously been a member of the Bionanotechnology and Interface's Lab in the Quantum Nano Centre in Waterloo, Ontario. Here, I explored the aggregation effects of different saline environments on DNA-adsorbed gold nanoparticles in varying Good's Buffers.

Biotechnology Enthusiast

I am currently a member of the Math and Modelling team for Waterloo iGEM (International Genetically Engineered Machine), a design team dedicated to the advancement of synthetic biology. Here, I worked on the rational protein design and molecular dynamics of the binding kinetics of a trypsin protein to phenylethylamine.

Community Outreach

I am currently working alongside Tech Tree Root founder, Scotty Shaw, to organize a global month-long datathon. With this experience, I hope to then go on and provide similar learning opportunities through hackathons for high school students in the Greater Toronto Area where I grew up.
